Pancake

Description

Fluffy and golden, pancakes are a breakfast favorite—delicious with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk 1 cup flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix 1 cup milk, 1 egg, and 2 tbsp melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat buttered skillet on medium. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Flip when bubbles form. Cook 1–2 minutes more.
  5. Serve with butter, syrup, or fresh fruit. Optional: add chocolate chips or blueberries to the batter before cooking.
