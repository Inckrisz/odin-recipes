Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread Slices
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter two bread slices. Place one slice, butter-side down, in a skillet.
  2. Add 2–3 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or mix). Top with second slice, butter-side up.
  3. Grill on medium until golden and cheese melts, about 2–3 minutes per side. Press slightly with spatula for even browning.
  4. Serve warm with tomato soup or pickles. Add extras like ham, tomato, or caramelized onions for variety.
