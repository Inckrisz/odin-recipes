Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ricotta
  • MOzarella
  • Parmezan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook 9 lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. In a pan, brown 1 lb ground beef with 1 chopped onion and 2 garlic cloves. Stir in 24 oz marinara sauce; simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Mix 15 oz ricotta with 1 egg, salt, pepper, and 1 cup shredded mozzarella.
  5. In a 9x13" dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, and repeat. Top with remaining mozzarella and 1/2 cup Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil; bake 25 minutes. Uncover and bake 10 more until bubbly and golden. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
