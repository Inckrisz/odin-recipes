Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

A rich, gooey chocolate cake made in minutes right in your mug—perfect for a sweet, single-serving dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. In a mug, mix 4 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp cocoa, 2 tbsp sugar, ¼ tsp baking powder, pinch salt.
  2. Add 3 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp oil, splash vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave 1 minute or until set. Let cool slightly.
  4. Add chocolate chips or peanut butter before microwaving for gooeyness. Top with ice cream or whipped cream if desired. Perfect for a quick sweet fix!
