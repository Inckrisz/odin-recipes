Guacamole

Description

A creamy avocado dip mixed with lime, tomato, onion, and spices—fresh, zesty, and great with chips or tacos.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic clove
  • Salt

Steps

  1. ash 3 ripe avocados. Mix in juice of 1 lime, 1 diced tomato, ¼ cup chopped red onion, 1 minced garlic clove, and salt. Add chopped cilantro and jalapeño to taste. Mix gently.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap directly on surface to avoid browning. Chill 30 minutes if desired.
  3. Serve with tortilla chips or on tacos, toast, or burgers. Customize with cumin, paprika, or diced mango for a twist.
